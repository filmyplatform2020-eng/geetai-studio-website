## 8 RULES FOR SCRIPTS THAT ACTUALLY GET WATCHED
1. Hook in first 3 seconds — or they're gone. Never start with your logo/intro.
2. One idea per video — don't try to teach 5 things in 30 seconds
3. Write for SOUND OFF first — captions/on-screen text carry 80% of viewers
4. End with a reason to comment/save/share — not just "follow me"
5. Keep cuts fast — change visual every 2-4 seconds to hold attention
6. Native platform feel beats polished — rough/authentic outperforms studio-quality
7. Post consistently — algorithm rewards frequency more than perfection
8. Re-hook at the 50% mark — re-engage viewers who are about to scroll
