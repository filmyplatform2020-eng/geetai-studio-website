## UNIVERSAL PROPOSAL STRUCTURE (use as base for any template below)

1. **Cover Page** — Client name, your brand, date, "Prepared for [Client] by [You]"
2. **Executive Summary** — 3-4 sentences: their challenge, your solution, expected outcome
3. **Understanding Your Needs** — Show you listened: restate their problem in your words
4. **Proposed Solution** — What you'll do, how, why this approach
5. **Scope & Deliverables** — Itemized list of exactly what's included
6. **Timeline** — Phases/milestones with dates
7. **Investment (Pricing)** — Table format (see pricing table templates below)
8. **Why Us** — Credentials, past results, differentiators (2-3 bullet points max)
9. **Terms** — Payment terms, validity period, what happens next
10. **Call to Action** — Clear next step: "Reply to confirm" / "Book a call to discuss" / signature line

## 7 RULES FOR PROPOSALS THAT CONVERT
1. Send within 24-48 hours of the conversation — momentum matters
2. Lead with THEIR problem, not your company history
3. Make pricing scannable — tables beat paragraphs
4. Always include 3 options (anchor pricing — middle option usually wins)
5. Add urgency naturally — limited slots, rate changes, seasonal timing
6. End with ONE clear next step — not multiple vague CTAs
7. Follow up within 3 days if no response — most deals close on follow-up #2-3
