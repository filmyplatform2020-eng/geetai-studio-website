## SECTION 1 — PROSPECT DISCOVERY (15 prompts)

1. "Generate a list of 20 company types in [industry] that would benefit from [your solution], ranked by likelihood of need."
2. "I sell [product/service] to [target customer]. List 15 specific job titles who would be the buyer or champion for this purchase."
3. "What are 10 signals (on a website, LinkedIn, or job posting) that indicate a company needs [your solution]?"
4. "List 20 niches within [broad industry] that are most likely to have [specific pain point] right now."
5. "Generate 15 search queries I could use on LinkedIn/Google to find [target persona] in [location/industry]."
6. "What online communities, forums, or groups would [target persona] likely be active in? List 10."
7. "Based on [target company profile], what departments/teams within the company would most need [your solution]?"
8. "List 10 trigger events (funding round, new hire, product launch, etc.) that signal a company is ready to buy [solution type]."
9. "What companies similar to [example company] might also be good prospects? List 15 with reasoning."
10. "Generate a list of 20 'ideal customer' attributes I should screen for when researching [target market]."
11. "What hashtags or keywords would [target persona] use that I could monitor for buying signals?"
12. "List 10 events, conferences, or webinars where [target persona] would likely attend."
13. "What podcasts, newsletters, or publications does [target persona] likely follow? List 10."
14. "Generate 15 LinkedIn search filter combinations to find [specific persona] at [company size/industry]."
15. "What are 10 ways to find decision-makers' contact info ethically and efficiently for [target company type]?"

## SECTION 2 — QUALIFICATION & RESEARCH (15 prompts)

16. "Given this company description: [paste], assess: do they likely need [your solution]? Score 1-10 with reasoning."
17. "What questions should I ask to qualify whether [prospect type] is a good-fit customer for [solution]?"
18. "Based on this LinkedIn profile/bio: [paste], what pain points might this person have related to [your solution area]?"
19. "Build a BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) qualification checklist for [your solution] sold to [target market]."
20. "What red flags suggest a prospect is NOT a good fit for [your solution]? List 10."
21. "Given this company's recent news/announcement: [paste], how could I use this as a conversation starter?"
22. "What's the likely budget range for [solution type] for a company of [size] in [industry]?"
23. "Generate 10 discovery call questions to uncover [target persona]'s real pain points around [topic]."
24. "Based on [industry] trends in [year], what challenges are companies like [target] likely facing right now?"
25. "What competitive solutions might [target company] currently be using, and how does [your solution] compare?"
26. "Build a lead-scoring rubric for [your solution] — what factors indicate high vs low likelihood to buy?"
27. "Given this job posting: [paste], what does it reveal about the company's current priorities/pain points?"
28. "What internal stakeholders typically need to approve a purchase of [solution type] at companies of [size]?"
29. "Generate a research checklist — what should I know about a prospect before reaching out?"
30. "Based on [company]'s public information, draft 3 hypotheses about their current challenges with [topic]."

## SECTION 3 — OUTREACH PREP & PERSONALIZATION (10 prompts)

31. "Given this prospect's recent LinkedIn post: [paste], suggest 3 ways to reference it naturally in an opener."
32. "What's a compelling one-line value proposition for [your solution] tailored to [specific persona/industry]?"
33. "Generate 5 personalized icebreakers for reaching out to someone in [role] at a [industry] company."

35. "What objections is [persona] likely to raise about [your solution], and how should I preemptively address them?"
36. "Draft 3 versions of an opening line referencing [specific company news/trigger event]."
37. "What tone and style of outreach works best for [persona type] — formal, casual, direct, consultative?"
38. "Generate a list of credibility signals (case studies, certifications, results) I should mention to [persona type]."
39. "What's the ideal outreach cadence (channels + timing) for reaching [persona] at [company type]?"
40. "Based on [prospect's role], what specific business outcome should my pitch emphasize most?"

46. "What metrics should I track to know if my lead-gen approach for [solution] is working?"

48. "What's a good cadence for re-engaging leads who went cold [X months] ago?"
49. "How should I structure a referral-request system to generate warm leads from existing customers?"
50. "Build a 30-day lead generation action plan for [your solution] targeting [market], assuming [X hours/week] available."
