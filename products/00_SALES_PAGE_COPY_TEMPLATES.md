## TONE RULES FOR ALL LISTING COPY
1. Lead with the OUTCOME the buyer gets, not the format ("templates," "PDF") — format is secondary
2. Be specific about numbers — "60 templates" beats "a bunch of templates" every time
3. Speak to ONE specific frustration per vault — broad copy converts no one
4. Keep paragraphs short — sales pages get skimmed, not read top to bottom
5. End every section with momentum toward the CTA — don't let attention drift
