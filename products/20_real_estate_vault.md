## SECTION 1 — PROPERTY LISTING DESCRIPTION FRAMEWORK

**4-part structure that sells the lifestyle, not just specs:**
1. **Opening hook** — paint the feeling of living there ("Imagine waking up to [specific feature/view/atmosphere]...")
2. **Standout features** — 3-4 things that make THIS property different (not a generic spec dump)
3. **Lifestyle/location context** — what's nearby, what the neighborhood offers, who it's perfect for
4. **Clear next step** — how to schedule a viewing/get more info

**Example skeleton:**
"[Opening hook tied to the property's best feature]. This [property type] offers [standout feature 1], [standout feature 2], and [standout feature 3] — perfect for [type of buyer/lifestyle]. Located in [area], you're just [X mins] from [key landmarks/amenities]. [Clear CTA — 'Schedule a viewing today' / 'Contact us to learn more']."

## 6 RULES FOR REAL ESTATE OUTREACH THAT BUILDS TRUST FAST
1. Sell the lifestyle first, specs second — buyers connect with how a place will make them feel
2. Always personalize to their stated criteria — generic property blasts get ignored
3. Respond fast — real estate moves quickly, and slow responses lose deals
4. Be the calm, informed guide — buying/selling is stressful; your steadiness builds trust
5. Follow up consistently but respectfully — most deals close after multiple touches, not the first one
6. Always give a clear next step — "let me know if interested" is weaker than "want me to schedule a viewing this week?"
