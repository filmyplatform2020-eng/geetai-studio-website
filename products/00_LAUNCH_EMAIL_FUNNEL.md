## SEQUENCING RULE
Don't send all 10 emails to a cold list at once. Pre-launch (1-3) → warm/build the list. Launch (4-5) → biggest push. Mid-launch (6-7) → re-engage openers/non-purchasers. Last-call (8-9) → urgency to remaining non-buyers only. Post-launch (10+) → evergreen nurture for new subscribers going forward.
