## SECTION 4 — AUDIENCE-ANGLE CHEAT SHEET
Same product, different angle per audience mindset:
- **Pain-point framing:** Lead with the cost/frustration of NOT having the solution
- **Aspiration framing:** Lead with the outcome/identity they want to achieve
- **Social-proof framing:** Lead with what similar people are already doing
- **Curiosity framing:** Lead with an unexpected fact/contrarian angle, reveal solution mid-copy

Test all 4 angles on the same offer — the winning angle often surprises you.

## 7 RULES FOR ADS THAT DON'T BURN BUDGET
1. Hook in the first 5 words of the headline — scanners decide instantly
2. One offer, one audience, one ad — don't try to speak to everyone
3. Match ad copy to landing page message — mismatches kill conversion
4. Always test 3+ variants — let data pick the winner, not your gut
5. Refresh creative every 1-2 weeks — ad fatigue is real and kills ROAS
6. Lead with the outcome, not the feature — "save 5 hours/week" beats "AI-powered automation"
7. Track cost-per-result, not just clicks — clicks that don't convert are wasted spend
