## PLATFORM ADAPTATION CHEAT SHEET
- **LinkedIn:** Add line breaks, end with a question, 1-3 hashtags max, professional tone
- **Twitter/X:** Punchier, thread-friendly, 1-2 hashtags, conversational
- **Instagram:** Visual-first (caption supports image), emojis OK, 5-15 hashtags in first comment
- **Facebook:** Slightly longer-form OK, community-tone, ask questions to drive comments
- **WhatsApp Status/Broadcast:** Ultra-short, personal, direct CTA
- **YouTube/Reels descriptions:** Front-load value/hook in first line (gets cut off), include links below
