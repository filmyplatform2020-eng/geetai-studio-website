## 8 RULES FOR WHATSAPP MESSAGES THAT GET REPLIES
1. Keep it SHORT — WhatsApp is a mobile-first, glance-and-reply channel
2. Always use their name — generic "Dear Customer" kills warmth instantly
3. One question per message — multiple asks overwhelm and reduce replies
4. Use emojis sparingly — 1-2 max, matches the casual nature of the channel
5. Never send walls of text — break into short lines, easy to skim on a phone
6. Time it right — avoid early morning/late night; mid-morning to early evening works best
7. Always give an easy reply option — "Reply YES/NO" gets more responses than open questions
8. Automate the routine, personalize the important — use templates for updates, write fresh for sensitive conversations (complaints, negotiations)
