## SECTION 2 — 20 LONG-FORM OUTLINES (blog/newsletter/LinkedIn-article ready)

**Outline 1 — "The Ultimate Guide to [Topic]"**
- Hook: Surprising stat or relatable problem statement
- Section 1: Why this matters now (context/stakes)
- Section 2: The core framework/approach (your unique angle)
- Section 3: Step-by-step breakdown
- Section 4: Common mistakes to avoid
- Section 5: Real example/case application
- Close: Recap + single clear next step

**Outline 2 — "X Lessons From Y Experience"**
- Hook: One-line teaser of the most surprising lesson
- Intro: Brief context — what the experience was
- Lessons 1-5: Each with a short story + the takeaway + how readers can apply it
- Close: Tie lessons together into one core insight + invite discussion

**Outline 3 — "The Problem With [Common Practice] (And What To Do Instead)"**
- Hook: Bold claim or contrarian statement
- Section 1: Why the common practice seems right (acknowledge the logic)
- Section 2: Where it actually breaks down (with evidence/examples)
- Section 3: The better alternative — explained simply
- Section 4: How to make the switch
- Close: Reframe — what becomes possible once you change approach

**Outline 4 — "Case Study: How [X] Achieved [Result]"**
- Hook: The headline result, stated plainly
- Section 1: The starting point/challenge
- Section 2: The strategy/approach taken
- Section 3: Execution — what actually happened, step by step
- Section 4: The results — specific numbers/outcomes
- Section 5: What can be replicated — key takeaways for the reader
- Close: Invitation — "want help applying this to your situation?"
