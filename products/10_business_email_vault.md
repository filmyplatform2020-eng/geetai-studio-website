## 6 RULES FOR BUSINESS EMAILS THAT GET RESPONSES
1. One topic per email — multi-topic emails get half-read and half-answered
2. Put the ask/action item in the FIRST two lines — don't bury it in paragraph 4
3. Match formality to the relationship — overly formal feels cold, overly casual feels unprofessional in the wrong context
4. Always state the next step clearly — "let me know your thoughts" is weaker than "could you confirm by Friday?"
5. Read difficult emails twice before sending — tone is easy to misjudge in writing; soften where needed
6. Keep it skimmable — short paragraphs, bullet points where possible; busy people skim, they don't read
